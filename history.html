About Us

A brief history

Founded in 2026, this outreach initiative was created to address a growing need for clear, trustworthy technology guidance in an increasingly connected world. As digital tools became more deeply embedded in homes, infrastructure, and everyday life, many communities, especially those seeking stability and protection, were left without accessible, practical support.

Technology should remain a reliable asset, not a vulnerability.

What began as a focused effort to share timely security updates and technology news quickly evolved into a broader mission of delivering practical, easy to understand guidance that helps keep all critters and their devices safe, secure, and connected. Operating as a remote first organization, the initiative prioritizes flexibility, responsiveness, and respect for the customs and preferences of the communities it serves.

Today, the organization continues to provide awareness, resources, and actionable insights designed to strengthen digital resilience, protect critical systems, and ensure technology remains a reliable asset rather than a vulnerability.
